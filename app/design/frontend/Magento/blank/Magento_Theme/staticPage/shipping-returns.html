Shipping & Returns

Shipping Options

We ship everything to US addresses via UPS.
International orders via US Postal Service.

Domestic

We ship via United States Postal Service (USPS) or the United Parcel Service (UPS). You can track your UPS package directly with them at www.ups.com or by calling 1-800-742-5877 ( 1-800-PICK-UPS).

International

We ship exclusively through the United States Postal Service (USPS). For shipping information and delivery estimates please contact us directly. International shipments should allow for 6-8 weeks delivery time in order to be received and cleared by local customs. Customers will be responsible for any applicable local duties, fees or customs charges.
